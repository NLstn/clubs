# Getting Started with Clubs

Welcome! This guide will walk you through everything you need to know to get started with the Clubs platform as a brand new user.

## What is Clubs?

Clubs is a comprehensive club management platform that helps organizations manage their members, events, fines, shifts, and communications. Whether you're starting your own club or joining an existing one, this guide will help you get up and running quickly.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- **A modern web browser**: Chrome, Firefox, Safari, or Edge (latest version recommended)
- **A valid email address**: Required for account creation and notifications
- **Internet connection**: Stable connection for best experience

## Quick Start: Your First 5 Steps

### Step 1: Access the Application

First, you need to navigate to your Clubs instance. The URL will depend on your organization's setup:

- **Organization-hosted**: Your organization will provide a URL like `https://clubs.yourorganization.com`
- **Local development**: If you're a developer, see the [Local Development Guide](../Documentation/LocalDev.md)

!!! tip "Can't Find the URL?"
    Contact your organization's IT department or the person who invited you to Clubs for the correct URL.

### Step 2: Create Your Account

Once you've accessed the application:

1. **Click "Sign Up"** or **"Create Account"** on the login page
2. **Choose your registration method**:
   
   **Option A: Single Sign-On (SSO)**
   - You'll be redirected to a registration form
   - Fill in:
     - Email address
     - Password (must be at least 8 characters with uppercase, lowercase, number, and special character)
     - First name
     - Last name
   - Click **"Register"** or **"Sign Up"**
   - Check your email for a verification link
   - Click the verification link to activate your account

   **Option B: Organization SSO**
   - Some organizations use their existing authentication system
   - Click on your organization's login button
   - Use your existing organization credentials
   - Your account will be created automatically

3. **Return to the login page** and sign in with your new credentials

!!! note "Email Verification"
    You must verify your email address before you can fully use the platform. Check your spam folder if you don't see the verification email within a few minutes.

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile

After your first login, take a moment to complete your profile:

1. **Click your profile icon** in the top navigation bar (usually in the top-right corner)
2. **Select "My Profile"** from the dropdown menu
3. **Click "Edit Profile"**
4. **Fill in your information**:
   - Verify your first and last name
   - Add a phone number (optional but recommended)
   - Add a short bio (optional)
   - Upload a profile picture (optional)
5. **Click "Save"** to update your profile

### Step 4: Join or Create a Club

Now you're ready to join the club management features!

#### Option A: Join an Existing Club

If you were invited to join a club or want to join an available club:

1. **Navigate to "Clubs"** in the main menu
   - Look for a navigation item labeled "Clubs" in the sidebar or top menu
2. **Browse available clubs**:
   - You'll see a list of public clubs you can join
   - Click on a club name to view its details
3. **Request to join**:
   - Click the **"Join Club"** or **"Request to Join"** button
   - Add a message explaining why you want to join (optional but recommended)
   - Click **"Submit"**
4. **Wait for approval**:
   - Club administrators will review your request
   - You'll receive a notification when your request is approved or denied

!!! tip "Can't See Any Clubs?"
    If no clubs are listed, either:
    - Your organization hasn't created any public clubs yet
    - You need an invitation to join (contact the club administrator)
    - You need to create your own club (see Option B below)

#### Option B: Create Your Own Club

If you want to start a new club:

1. **Navigate to "Clubs"** in the main menu
2. **Click "Create Club"** or **"New Club"** button
   - This button is typically at the top of the clubs list
3. **Fill in club details**:
   - **Club Name**: Choose a clear, descriptive name (e.g., "Mountain Biking Club", "Chess Society")
   - **Description**: Explain what your club is about, who can join, and what activities you'll do
   - **Privacy Setting**: 
     - **Public**: Anyone can see and request to join your club
     - **Private**: Only invited members can see and join
4. **Click "Save"** or **"Create Club"**

### Step 5: Set Up Your Club (For Club Creators)

If you just created a club, here are the essential first steps:

1. **Invite your first members**:
   - Go to your club page
   - Click **"Admin"** or **"Manage Club"**
   - Navigate to **"Invitations"** or **"Members"**
   - Click **"Invite Member"**
   - Enter email addresses and send invitations

2. **Create your first event** (optional):
   - In the admin panel, go to **"Events"**
   - Click **"Add Event"** or **"Create Event"**
   - Fill in event details (name, date, time, location)
   - Click **"Save"**

3. **Post a welcome message**:
   - In the admin panel, go to **"News"**
   - Click **"Add News"** or **"Create Post"**
   - Write a welcome message introducing the club
   - Click **"Publish"**

## Common First-Time User Questions

### "I logged in, but now what?"

After logging in, you should see your dashboard. The dashboard shows:
- Your upcoming events
- Recent news from your clubs
- Outstanding fines (if any)
- Your assigned shifts
- Quick links to common actions

Start by joining a club or creating one using the steps above.

### "I can't find the Clubs menu"

Look for navigation in these common locations:
- **Sidebar** (left side of the screen)
- **Top menu bar** (horizontal menu at the top)
- **Hamburger menu** (three horizontal lines icon, usually top-left on mobile)

If you still can't find it, you may need to click a menu icon to expand the navigation.

### "My join request wasn't approved"

Join requests require manual approval from club administrators. This process can take:
- Minutes to hours if administrators are active
- 1-2 days if administrators check periodically
- Longer if the club is not actively managed

If it's been more than a few days, try:
- Contacting the club administrator directly
- Checking if there are specific requirements to join
- Looking for alternative clubs

### "I received an invitation but don't see it"

If you were invited to join a club:
1. Check your email for the invitation link
2. Click the link in the email (it will take you directly to accept)
3. If the link expired, ask the administrator to resend the invitation

## Still Need Help?

### Troubleshooting Common Issues

**"I can't access the application"**
- Verify you have the correct URL
- Check your internet connection
- Try a different browser
- Clear your browser cache and cookies
- Contact your organization's IT support

**"I didn't receive the verification email"**
- Check your spam/junk folder
- Verify you entered the correct email address
- Wait a few minutes and check again
- Request a new verification email from the login page

**"I forgot my password"**
- Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
- Enter your email address
- Check your email for a password reset link
- Follow the instructions to create a new password

**"The application is slow or not loading"**
- Check your internet connection
- Try refreshing the page
- Clear your browser cache
- Try a different browser
- Check if others are experiencing the same issue
