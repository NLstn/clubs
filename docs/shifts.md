# Shift Scheduling

This guide explains how to create, manage, and participate in shift schedules within your club.

## Understanding Shifts

Shifts are scheduled time slots where members perform specific duties or tasks. They're useful for:
- Event staffing
- Facility management
- Equipment monitoring
- Volunteer coordination
- Rotation of responsibilities

Each shift includes:
- Shift name and description
- Date and time
- Assigned member(s)
- Location
- Required number of people
- Shift status

## Viewing Shifts

### Your Assigned Shifts

To view your upcoming shifts:

1. **Navigate to Dashboard**: Go to your main dashboard
2. **Check "My Shifts"**: See all shifts assigned to you
3. **View Shift Details**: Click on a shift to see:
   - Shift date and time
   - Location
   - Duration
   - Description and responsibilities
   - Other assigned members (if any)

### Club Shift Schedule

To view all club shifts:

1. **Navigate to Your Club**: Select your club
2. **Click "Shifts"**: View the complete shift schedule
3. **Browse Schedule**: See all past and upcoming shifts
4. **Filter Shifts**: Filter by date, status, or member

## Managing Shifts (Administrators)

As a club administrator, you can create and manage the shift schedule.

### Creating Shifts

To create a new shift:

1. **Open Admin Panel**: Navigate to your club's admin section
2. **Go to "Shifts"**: Select the shift management page
3. **Click "Add Shift"**: Start creating a new shift
4. **Fill in Shift Details**:
   - **Title**: Name of the shift (e.g., "Morning Door Duty")
   - **Description**: Detailed responsibilities and tasks
   - **Date and Time**: When the shift starts
   - **Duration**: How long the shift lasts
   - **Location**: Where the shift takes place
   - **Required Members**: Number of people needed
5. **Assign Members**: Select which member(s) will work this shift
6. **Save Shift**: Click save to create the shift

!!! success "Shift Created"
    Assigned members will be notified about their new shift.

### Editing Shifts

To modify an existing shift:

1. **Go to Shifts List**: Navigate to the admin shifts page
2. **Select Shift**: Click on the shift you want to edit
3. **Make Changes**: Update any shift details
4. **Reassign Members**: Change who's assigned if needed
5. **Save Changes**: Don't forget to save your modifications

!!! note "Member Notifications"
    Members are notified when their shift assignments change.

### Deleting Shifts

If you need to cancel a shift:

1. **Navigate to Shift**: Go to the shift in admin panel
2. **Select "Delete Shift"**: Choose the delete option
3. **Confirm Deletion**: Assigned members will be notified
4. **Save**: Confirm the deletion

## Shift Assignment Strategies

### Direct Assignment

Assign specific members to shifts:
- Best for regular duties
- Ensures accountability
- Clear responsibility

**How to:**
1. Create the shift
2. Select member(s) from the member list
3. Save the assignment

### Rotating Schedules

Distribute shifts fairly among members:
- Share responsibility equally
- Prevent burnout
- Ensure everyone participates

**How to:**
1. Create multiple shifts over time
2. Assign different members to each
3. Track participation to ensure fairness

### Team-Based Assignments

Assign entire teams to shifts:
- Good for group activities
- Maintains team cohesion
- Simplifies scheduling

**How to:**
1. Organize members into teams
2. Create shifts and assign teams
3. Team members work together

- Post-shift completion confirmation

## Shift Status Tracking

### Shift States

Shifts can have different statuses:

**Scheduled**: Shift is planned and assigned
**In Progress**: Shift is currently happening
**Completed**: Shift has been finished
**Missed**: Assigned member didn't show up
**Cancelled**: Shift was cancelled

### Tracking Completion

Mark shifts as completed:

1. **View Shift**: Navigate to the shift details
2. **Mark Complete**: Update status after the shift
3. **Add Notes**: Include any relevant observations
4. **Save**: Record the completion

### Handling Missed Shifts

If a member misses their shift:

1. **Update Status**: Mark the shift as missed
2. **Document Reason**: Add notes about the absence
3. **Follow Up**: Contact the member
4. **Consider Fine**: Apply fine if per club policy

## Best Practices

### Creating Effective Shift Schedules

1. **Plan Ahead**: Create shifts well in advance
2. **Clear Instructions**: Provide detailed shift descriptions
3. **Reasonable Duration**: Keep shifts to manageable lengths
4. **Fair Distribution**: Rotate shifts among members
5. **Flexibility**: Allow shift swaps when needed

### Shift Descriptions

Include in shift descriptions:
- Specific tasks and responsibilities
- Required equipment or materials
- Contact information for emergencies
- Handoff procedures (if applicable)
- Special instructions or notes

### Scheduling Tips

1. **Regular Patterns**: Use consistent scheduling patterns
2. **Member Availability**: Consider member schedules
3. **Skill Matching**: Assign shifts based on member abilities
4. **Backup Plans**: Have backup members identified
5. **Balance Workload**: Ensure no member is over-scheduled

### Communication

1. **Advance Notice**: Give members plenty of warning
2. **Confirmation**: Confirm shift acceptance
3. **Reminders**: Send timely reminders before shifts
4. **Updates**: Communicate any changes promptly
5. **Feedback**: Collect feedback on shift experiences

## Shift Swaps and Changes

### Requesting Shift Changes

If you can't make your shift:

1. **Notify Early**: Contact administrator as soon as possible
2. **Find Coverage**: Help find a replacement if you can
3. **Official Request**: Request the change through the system
4. **Wait for Approval**: Administrator must approve changes

### Approving Shift Swaps

As an administrator:

1. **Review Request**: Check the swap request details
2. **Verify Coverage**: Ensure replacement is suitable
3. **Approve or Deny**: Make a decision
4. **Update Schedule**: System updates automatically
5. **Notify Members**: Both members are informed

## Troubleshooting

### Can't Create Shifts?

- Verify you have administrator privileges
- Check that you're in the correct club section
- Ensure all required fields are filled

### Shift Not Appearing?

- Refresh the page
- Check date filters
- Verify the shift wasn't deleted

### Member Not Receiving Notifications?

- Check member's notification settings
- Verify email address is correct
- Check spam/junk folders

### Can't Assign Member to Shift?

- Ensure member is active in the club
- Check if member has scheduling conflicts
- Verify you have permission to assign shifts

### Shift Times Incorrect?

- Check timezone settings
- Verify date and time entry
- Ensure clock is synchronized

### Need Help?

Contact your club administrator or system administrator for assistance with shift-related issues.
