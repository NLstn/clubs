# Member Management

This guide explains how to manage members in your club, including inviting new members, managing roles, and handling join requests.

## Viewing Members

To view your club's members:

1. **Navigate to Your Club**: Select your club from the clubs list
2. **Click "Members"**: View the complete list of club members
3. **View Member Details**: Click on a member to see their profile and club activity

### Member Information

The members list shows:
- Member name and email
- Join date
- Role (Administrator or Member)
- Team assignments
- Recent activity

## Inviting Members

As a club administrator, you can invite new members:

1. **Open Admin Panel**: Navigate to your club's admin section
2. **Go to "Invitations"**: Select the invitations management page
3. **Click "Invite Member"**: Start the invitation process
4. **Enter Member Details**:
   - Email address of the person to invite
   - Optional personal message
   - Select initial role (Member or Administrator)
5. **Send Invitation**: Click send to email the invitation

!!! note "Invitation Validity"
    Invitations are valid for a limited time. If an invitation expires, you'll need to send a new one.

### Managing Pending Invitations

View and manage sent invitations:

1. **View Pending Invites**: See all invitations that haven't been accepted yet
2. **Resend Invitation**: Send the invitation email again if needed
3. **Cancel Invitation**: Revoke an invitation before it's accepted

## Join Requests

When someone requests to join your club, you'll need to review and approve their request:

1. **Navigate to Join Requests**: In the admin panel, select "Join Requests"
2. **Review Requests**: See a list of pending join requests
3. **View Requester Profile**: Click on a request to see more details
4. **Approve or Deny**:
   - **Approve**: Accept the member into your club
   - **Deny**: Reject the request with optional feedback

!!! tip "Tip"
    Review join requests promptly to maintain a positive experience for potential members.

## Managing Member Roles

### Understanding Roles

**Administrator**:
- Can manage all aspects of the club
- Can invite and remove members
- Can create and manage events, fines, shifts, and teams
- Can modify club settings
- Can promote and demote members

**Member**:
- Can view club information
- Can participate in events and activities
- Can view news and receive notifications
- Limited management capabilities

### Changing Member Roles

To promote or demote a member:

1. **Go to Members List**: Navigate to the admin members page
2. **Select a Member**: Click on the member whose role you want to change
3. **Change Role**: Use the role selector to change their role
4. **Confirm**: Save the changes

!!! warning "Be Careful"
    Administrators have full access to club management. Only promote trusted members.

## Removing Members

If you need to remove a member from your club:

1. **Navigate to Members**: Go to the admin members page
2. **Select Member**: Find the member you want to remove
3. **Click "Remove Member"**: Use the remove option
4. **Confirm Removal**: Confirm that you want to remove the member

!!! note "Note"
    Removed members can request to join again if the club is public. Consider privacy settings if needed.

## Best Practices

### Building Your Member Base

1. **Start Small**: Begin with a core group of engaged members
2. **Clear Expectations**: Communicate club rules and expectations
3. **Regular Communication**: Keep members informed through news posts
4. **Recognize Contributions**: Acknowledge active and helpful members

### Managing a Growing Club

1. **Promote Helper Admins**: As your club grows, promote trusted members to help manage
2. **Organize into Teams**: Use teams to better organize large groups
3. **Regular Check-ins**: Monitor member activity and engagement
4. **Handle Requests Promptly**: Respond quickly to join requests and member issues

### Member Retention

1. **Create Engaging Events**: Regular activities keep members interested
2. **Foster Community**: Encourage member interaction and participation
3. **Be Responsive**: Answer questions and address concerns quickly
4. **Gather Feedback**: Ask members for input on club activities and decisions

## Troubleshooting

### Invitation Not Received?

- Check spam/junk folders
- Verify the email address is correct
- Resend the invitation if needed
- Contact the invitee directly with the club name

### Can't Remove a Member?

- Ensure you have administrator privileges
- Check if there are any dependencies (pending fines, upcoming shifts)
- Contact system administrator if issues persist

### Member Role Not Updating?

- Refresh the page to see changes
- Verify you saved the changes
- Check that you have permission to change roles

### Need Help?

Contact your system administrator for assistance with member management issues.
